# Capsule Filter Technology: Innovation in Tobacco Filtration

Capsule filter technology has revolutionized the cigarette industry by giving consumers control over their smoking experience. This innovative technology allows smokers to customize flavor intensity with a simple crush of a capsule embedded within the filter.

## How Capsule Filters Work

A capsule filter contains one or more small beads (capsules) filled with flavoring liquid. These capsules are:

1. **Encased in a crushable shell** - typically made from gelatin or plant-based materials
2. **Positioned within the filter** - strategically placed for optimal release
3. **Activated by consumer** - crushed between fingers during smoking

When crushed, the capsule releases its contents, instantly transforming the smoking experience by adding flavor to the smoke passing through the filter.

## Technical Considerations

### Capsule Specifications
- **Diameter**: 2.5mm - 4.0mm
- **Shell thickness**: Optimized for desired crush force
- **Fill volume**: Determines flavor intensity
- **Placement**: Can be center, offset, or near mouth-end

### Crush Force
The force required to break the capsule is carefully calibrated:
- Too soft: Accidental crushing during handling
- Too hard: Difficult for consumers to activate
- Optimal: 300-500g crush force

### Flavor Stability
Our capsules maintain flavor integrity through:
- UV-resistant materials
- Moisture barriers
- Optimized storage conditions
